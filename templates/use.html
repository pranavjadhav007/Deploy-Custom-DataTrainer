Custom Data Trainer

    How to use:

  1. Gather the pictures you wish to use to train the model.
  2. Make new Folder named FolderTrain in google drive and make its visiblity public from share option.
  3. Upload the collected images in the given format:
    1. Make two folder named Training and Validation


    2. Inside training and validation folder make seperate folders for each class or label. (For eg: if want to train model for Classification of diffent denominations then make seperate folders named 300_notes, 500_notes etc inside training and validation folder both.)
       


    3. Upload all your images into their respective classes or labels in both. (For eg: all the 500rs notes in 500_notes folder.)
  4. Copy the FolderTrain Link from share option.
